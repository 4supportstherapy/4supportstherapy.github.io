New Free Guide: "Counselling Services in Context" - For Kenyan HR, Schools & NGOs

Published: June 13, 2026 | By Hannah Wanjuhi Ticha

In October 2020, I was contracted to develop digital training content for counselling providers. The result was 4 practical modules covering the realities of delivering mental health support in Kenyan institutions.

I’m now sharing the consolidated report free because too many organizations want to support staff and students but don’t know where to start.

What’s Inside the Guide?

  • Workplace Counselling: Induction, team building, stress & burnout management, psychoeducation
  • Educational Institutions: Student guidance, handling indiscipline, drug abuse awareness, group sessions
  • Crisis Counselling: PTSD assessment, trauma response, using DSM-5 in Kenyan settings
  • Management of Services: Ethics, documentation, KCPA compliance, counsellor supervision & wellbeing

You’ll also find the 5 Stages of the Counselling Process with real Kenyan case examples — from workplace burnout to teen pregnancy during COVID.

This is for HR managers, school principals, NGO program officers, and counsellors who need a clear, field-tested framework for setting up services that actually work.
