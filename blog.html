My Journey & Expertise

As a Counselling and Educational/Psycho-pedagogy professional, I bring years of dedicated experience in one-on-one and group counselling. I specialize in:

  • Marriage & Family Therapy
  • Adolescent Counselling
  • SGBV & Trauma Counselling
  • HTS (HIV Testing Services) Counselling

Beyond the therapy room, I’m a motivational speaker and guidance counsellor serving schools, educational institutions, churches, and conferences. My background as a high school English & Literature teacher sharpened my passion for language, public speaking, and connecting with people.

Specialized Training

I am a trained practitioner in the Therapeutic Community Model (TCM) for behavioral modification in both criminal and addictive behavior. As a Trainer of Trainers (TOT) expert, I have vast experience facilitating training that empowers others to make lasting change.

Community Heart

I’m deeply committed to community service. I actively support people with developmental disabilities through Life Skills Training geared toward personal development and, ultimately, meaningful work and independence.

My Passion & Vision

My passion is to be part of the team that ensures improved and sustained psychosocial and spiritual well-being for disadvantaged populations and vulnerable communities. I’m committed to promoting employees’ personal development, motivation, and productivity with respect to gender equity — in Kenya and internationally.

I intend to further my studies and engage in research in Counselling Psychology to fully exploit my potential in this discipline I love.
